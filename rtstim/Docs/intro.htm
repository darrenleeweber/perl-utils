RTStim

 
 

        RTStim 2.0 is a hard-real-time application for Neuroimaging and Neuropsychology experimentation. RTStim runs on a RTAI 24.1.11 patched Linux 2.4.20 kernel. RTStim is well suited for precise MEG, FMRI and EEG experimentation. The system makes use of the RTAI real-time executive to obtain hard-real-time scheduling. Low-latency is achieved by writing our own kernel-space drivers  for the peripherals. A RME audio card and Radeon 7500 or 9000 AGP DVI graphics card are selected to guarantee the low-latency hardware performance.

     This document covers the use and installation of RTStim. The following table lists the contents of the RTStim 2.0 release. Please see  the RTStim website at:  http://www.unm.edu/~sheltraw/rtstim/    where one can download RTStim,  obtain news about RTStim and subscribe to the RTStim mailing list.
